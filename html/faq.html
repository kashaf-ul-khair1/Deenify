Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Deenify — from features to our vision.

✦
What is Deenify? +
Deenify is an all-in-one Islamic companion app designed to help Muslims stay connected to their Deen in their busy daily lives. It brings together prayer times, Quran, Hadith, Zakat calculator, Dhikr counter, and Islamic calendar in one clean and easy-to-use interface.
How are prayer times calculated? +
Prayer times are calculated using your device's GPS location combined with standard Islamic astronomical algorithms. The app supports multiple calculation methods including ISNA, MWL, Karachi, and Egypt, so you can choose the method most appropriate for your region and school of thought.
Which Quran translations are available? +
Deenify offers multiple Quran translations in English, Urdu, and other major languages. English translations include those by Sahih International, Pickthall, Yusuf Ali, and Dr. Mustafa Khattab. The full Arabic text is always available alongside any translation.
Are the Hadith sources authentic? +
Yes. All Hadith in Deenify are sourced from the six canonical Hadith collections (Kutub al-Sittah): Sahih al-Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, Sunan Abu Dawud, Sunan at-Tirmidhi, Sunan an-Nasa'i, and Sunan Ibn Majah. Each Hadith includes its chain of narration and authenticity grade where applicable.
How does the Zakat calculator work? +
You enter your assets — cash, gold, silver, business inventory, investments, and receivables — and the calculator applies the current Nisab threshold and the standard 2.5% Zakat rate. The Nisab values are updated based on current gold and silver market prices, ensuring your calculation is always accurate.
What inspired the creation of Deenify? +
The idea was born from a shared concern among our team: that Muslims, especially young people, are gradually drifting away from their religious practice due to the demands of modern life. We wanted to create a digital tool that does not just inform but actually facilitates practice — making it easier to pray on time, read Quran regularly, and stay connected to Islamic values every single day.
Is Deenify free to use? +
This project is currently an ICT course prototype. All features showcased on this website represent our vision for a fully developed app. Our goal has always been to make Deenify freely accessible to Muslims worldwide, without paywalls or subscriptions for essential Islamic tools.
Who built Deenify? +
Deenify was designed and developed by Tayyaba Zubaid and Kashaf-ul-khair Khalid as part of their ICT project at COMSATS University. The project was guided by their instructor and represents many weeks of collaboration, research, and user feedback from the Muslim community.
Can I suggest new features? +
Absolutely. We welcome all feedback and suggestions. Please use the Contact page to reach out directly to our team. We carefully read every message and incorporate community feedback into our development roadmap.
