FAQs

How do you ensure the app is aligned with Islamic principles and teachings?

We consult with Islamic scholars during every stage of development to ensure all features, content, and guidelines adhere to Sharia principles. Additionally, we allow users to submit feedback and questions to ensure inclusivity and accuracy.

What trends in the Muslim population or social media usage support your app's potential success?

With a growing global Muslim population, currently over 1.9 billion, and the increasing use of smartphones and social media platforms in Muslim-majority countries, there’s a clear opportunity to engage this audience digitally. Reports show that religious apps are among the top downloads in these regions.

What is your plan for capturing users in regions with limited internet access or technology adoption?

Our app will be completely offline in order to work in regions with limited internet access.

How do you plan to generate revenue? Are there in-app purchases or premium features?

The app is completely free to download. We only wanted to help our Muslim community in struggling with their religion norms.

How will the app encourage consistent use and adherence to religious practices?

Daily reminders, motivational quotes from the Quran and Hadith and rewards for consistency will encourage regular use. Users can also set personal goals to track their spiritual journey.

What steps will you take if the app receives negative feedback from scholars or users?

We will treat feedback as an opportunity to improve, consulting with scholars and implementing necessary changes to address concerns while keeping the app user-friendly.

How do you envision the app contributing to the global Muslim community?

By promoting spiritual growth, fostering a global community, and making Islamic practices more accessible, the app can help Muslims worldwide stay connected to their faith.
