What We Built

App Features

Every tool in Deenify is crafted to support your spiritual life — from Fajr to Isha and everything in between.

🕌 Prayer Times
Core Feature

Precise Prayer Times & Qibla

Never miss a Salah again. Deenify calculates accurate prayer times for your exact location using reliable astronomical algorithms.

  • Location-based Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha times
  • Customizable Adhan reminders with multiple sounds
  • Qibla direction compass using device sensors
  • Hijri date displayed alongside Gregorian
📖 Holy Quran
Spiritual Growth

Quran Reader & Audio

Explore the Holy Quran with clarity and depth. Read in Arabic, follow the translation, and listen to world-renowned reciters.

  • Full Arabic text with multiple translations
  • Audio recitation by famous Qaris
  • Bookmark verses and track reading progress
  • Verse-by-verse Tafsir and commentary
🌙 Hadith
Prophetic Wisdom

Hadith of the Day

Start each day with wisdom from the Prophet ﷺ. Browse authentic Hadith collections with grading, context, and explanation.

  • Collections from Bukhari, Muslim, Abu Dawud, and more
  • Daily push notification with a new Hadith
  • Search and filter by topic or narrator
  • Share Hadith easily with family and friends
🧮 Zakat
Financial Worship

Zakat Calculator

Fulfil one of Islam's Five Pillars with confidence. Our Zakat calculator handles all major asset types with current Nisab values.

  • Gold, silver, cash, and savings calculation
  • Business inventory and receivables
  • Real-time Nisab values based on gold/silver prices
  • Guidance on eligible recipients (Masarif)
📿 Tasbih
Daily Dhikr

Digital Tasbih & Dhikr

Build a daily habit of remembrance. Track your Dhikr with a beautiful counter that supports custom phrases, goals, and streaks.

  • One-tap counting with haptic feedback
  • Pre-loaded morning and evening Adhkar
  • Set daily targets and track your streak
  • Custom phrases for personal Dhikr routines
🗓️ Calendar
Islamic Time

Islamic Calendar & Events

Stay aware of the Hijri date and never miss important Islamic occasions — from Ramadan to Eid and beyond.

  • Full Hijri calendar with Gregorian sync
  • Key Islamic dates and observances
  • Ramadan countdown with Suhoor and Iftar times
  • Personal goal setting for Islamic milestones
